‚òï About Me

My name is Dimitris Sikeridis and I am a Ph.D. candidate at the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering, University of New Mexico, since August 2016. My advisor is professor Michael Devetsikiotis and I am a member of the UNM Internet of Things Lab.

In July 2016, I obtained my Diploma in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Patras. My Diploma Thesis was supervised by professor Theodore Antonakopoulos at the Laboratory of Electrotechnics, Cognitive Computing Machines and Embedded Systems (COMES) Group.
